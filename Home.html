WELCOME TO DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE


The Department of Computer Science at the University of Jaffna is one of the remarkable centres of teaching and research in Computer Science in Sri Lanka celebrating its 30 Years of Computing. There are currently over 330 undergraduate and few postgraduate students studying within the Department. The Department aims to collaborate in its activities with the IT industrial sector across the country in research and teaching.




VISION

Be a leading centre for quality education and high-end research in Computer Science

MISSION

To train intellectually and professionally competent graduates and postgraduates in alignment with current and emerging needs of the national and international community, engage in research and innovation and research commercialization, promote scholarship and engage with industry and community with a special emphasis on the social, economic and cultural needs of Northern Sri Lanka
